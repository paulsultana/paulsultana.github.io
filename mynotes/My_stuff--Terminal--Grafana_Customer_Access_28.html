Grafana Customer Access


To add yourself to a customer's organization, simply run this command:

curl -X POST https://n8n-soc.wedos.global/webhook/grafana-add-viewer \

-H "Content-Type: application/json" \

-d '{"wgp_id": "wgp415167"}'

Just replace wgp415167 with the customer's WGP ID. After that, log in to Grafana and switch to their organization using the org switcher in the top left corner.
